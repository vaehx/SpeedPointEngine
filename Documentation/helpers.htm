Renderer: Helper objects

In many cases you may want to render helper geometry to debug your algorithms.
In SpeedPoint, you can do this very easily: 
#include <Abstract\I3DEngine.h>
...
CBoxHelper* pBox = p3DEngine->AddHelper<CBoxHelper>(CBoxHelper::Params(vMin, vMax));

To delete a helper, use `pBox->Release()`. The helper will be marked as trash and will be removed during the garbage collection phase of the renderer.
DO NOT access the pointer to the helper after releasing it!

Available helpers are:

  • CDynamicMeshHelper: Allows direct access to the RenderDesc of the helper
  • CPointHelper
  • CLineHelper
  • CBoxHelper
  • CSphereHelper
